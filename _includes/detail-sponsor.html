Sponsorship Packages

Every sponsorship gets:

  • a logo link on the event page
  • text link in promo emails
  • verbal recognition during the event
  • a handful of social posts about your company

As you might expect, higher sponsorship levels get bigger logos and more social posts.

$100We’ll give away your stickers, shirts or other swag on event day.
$250Everything above, plus: 1 free ticket to the event.
$500Everything above, plus: 2 free tickets, and your logo on a big sign at the event and in the screensaver slide rotation.
$750Everything above, plus: We’ll talk about your company on stage for a few minutes.
$1000Everything above, plus: A dedicated email to all attendees about your company, and a table where you can give your stuff away.
$1500Everything above, plus: Your logo on several big signs at the event, and on table tents during one of the activities.
$2000Everything above, plus: 4 free tickets, even more big signs, verbal thank you for all the drinks and snacks, 5 minutes on stage to tell a story about what you do.
$2500Everything above, plus: Mentions in the opening remarks, at each break, and in closing remarks, a co-branded Web Afternoon / Your Company t-shirt for every attendee, PLUS; we’ll work with you to create a special way to promote you at the event.
